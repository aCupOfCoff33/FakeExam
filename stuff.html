Mid-term exam on 02-Nov

Yimin Yang posted on Oct 30, 10:26 AM

We have a mid-term exam coming up. Here is some information as below:

  1. Mid-term exam is worth 20 percent of the total grade.
  2. The Mid-term exam duration will be 1 hour and 30 minutes at ACEB 1410 between 9:30 pm to 10:45 pm. However, you really only need 30 minutes or less to complete the test.
  3. Coverage: Everything from the first lecture to the lecture this week Wednesday. Hence, you need to know the following:
    • Introduction for JavaScript
    • HTML and JS in the browser
    • Node.JS with IDE
    • JavaScript Functions
    • JS Control Flow & Data Types
    • JS Objects & Object prototypes
    • JS "This" Keyword & Lexical Scopes and Closures
